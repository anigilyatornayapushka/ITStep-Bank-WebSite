1. General information

ItStepBank takes the protection of your privacy and the security of personal data seriously.This privacy policy describes what personal data we collect, why we hold it, how we process this data, whether we share it with third parties, how we use cookies and tracking technologies, the privacy protection options available, your rights in relation to personal data, the security measures in place to protect your personal information and other important information.

2. What personal data do we collect?

  • First and last name: We collect your full name to identify and contact you.
  • Contact Information: This includes your email address, that we use to contact you, send you notifications, and keep your account secure.
  • Financial Information: We may collect information about your financial accounts, credit or debit cards in order to facilitate payments and transactions within our payment system.

3. Why do we store Personal Data?

  • Provision and management of your account: We use your personal data to create and manage your account in our payment system so that you can make payments, transfers and other financial transactions.
  • Payment and Transaction Processing: Your personal data is used to process payments, transfers and other financial transactions that you initiate through our payment system.
  • Security: We use your personal data to ensure the security of your account, prevent fraud, detect and solve security problems and protect the interests of ItStepBank and our users.
  • Compliance with legislation: We may store your personal data in accordance with applicable laws, including data retention requirements for audit, taxation, accounting and compliance with financial regulations.

4. How do we process Personal Data?

We process your personal data in accordance with applicable data protection laws and our privacy policy. Processing includes the following actions:

  • Collection: We collect personal data that you provide to us when registering and using our payment system, as well as data obtained during your financial transactions.
  • Keeping: We store your personal data in a secure form on our servers or the servers of our partners in accordance with our security policies.
  • Using: We use your personal data to provide services, make payments and transactions, communicate with you, as well as for the purposes of analyzing, improving and developing our payment system.
  • Broadcast: We may transfer your personal data to third parties only in accordance with our privacy policy and applicable law, including when it is necessary to process payments, comply with legal requirements or protect the interests of ItStepBank and our users.
  • Analysis: We may analyze your personal data to determine usage trends , improve security, improve functionality and develop new services.

5. Do we share Personal Data?

We may transfer your personal data to third parties only in the following cases:

  • Approval: We may transfer your personal data to third parties with your consent or upon your request.
  • Financial Transactions: We may transfer your personal data to payment providers, banks and other participants in financial transactions as part of the processing and execution of your payments and transactions.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your personal data in response to requests from law enforcement agencies, court decisions, compliance with legislation or other legal requirements.

6. How do we use cookies and tracking technologies?

We use cookies and similar technologies for various purposes:

  • Improving functionality: We use cookies to ensure the proper functioning of our payment system, including saving your settings and preferences.
  • Analysis and Improvement: We use cookies to analyze user usage and behavior in order to improve our payment system, provide a personalized experience and offer more relevant services and recommendations.

7. How do we protect your Personal Data?

We take security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. We use technical, administrative and physical security measures such as data encryption, access control and monitoring to protect your personal data from loss, theft and unauthorized access.

8. What else do you need to know?

Our privacy policies may change from time to time in accordance with changes in our services and legal requirements. We recommend that you review the latest version of our privacy policy on our website from time to time.

9. Contact us

If you have any questions, comments or need additional information, please contact us via ItStepBank.

10. Definitions

Personal data
Any information relating to an identified or identifiable individual. Identifiable is an individual who can be identified directly (for example, by name, identifier) or indirectly through the use of additional information, which may include elements such as the physical, physiological, genetic, mental, economic, cultural or social identity of this individual.
Processing of personal data
Any action or set of actions performed with personal data, including collection, recording, organization, structuring, storage, adaptation, modification, extraction, consulting, use, disclosure by transmission, distribution or otherwise granting access, alignment or combination restriction, deletion or destruction of data.
Cookies
Small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website or use an application. They contain information about your activity and preferences and are used to improve functionality, analyze usage and provide a personalized experience.
Third parties
Persons or organizations that are not ItStepBank, including external service providers, payment processing partners, analytical services and advertising partners with whom ItStepBank may cooperate in the provision of services or processing of personal data.
Privacy Policy
A document that describes how ItStepBank collects, uses, stores and protects users' personal data, as well as regulates their processing and transfer to third parties.
ItStepBank Payment System
A payment system provided by ItStepBank that allows users to perform financial transactions, make payments, receive and send funds, as well as perform other actions related to financial transactions.
